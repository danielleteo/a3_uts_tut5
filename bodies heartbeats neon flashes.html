prologue

bodies heartbeats neon flashes

bodies heartbeats neon flashes on the dance floor.

there is a haze drowning the entire room, the barely-corporeal shapes intoxicated – if not by the spirits burning their throats, then those emanating from the room in an engulfing energy.

the collective thrum of lost souls echoes in the blood and bass, reverberating off the walls and sealing the darkened room in a vacuum of empty sound.

there is no self; not tonight. you are absolved amongst the nameless mass – bodies heartbeats neon flashes on the dance floor.
