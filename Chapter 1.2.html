"Hey, Boss."

Jaz struts into the room, wearing his signature suit and grin.

"Heard we had a name-caller."

He flashes his teeth in wolfish fashion, the boyish façade overturned, as he sinks into the plush leather seat.

Nex glances over at Jaz, no more disdain than usual in the look.

"She threw him out," he says, voice low, as Ezri scrawls the serial number on a slip and slides it towards him. "Physically."

Jaz cards a hand through his messy golden locks, making a face as if scalded by hot water.
"Bet that did not go down well for him."

The apparent sympathy dissolves into a chuckle. "So, what will it be this time, Boss? A little shake-and-snatch, maybe some light torture?" He waggles his eyebrows.

Ezri rolls her eyes but lets the corner of her mouth lift slightly.
"Talk to the usual suspects. Especially Avon," she adds when Jaz opens his mouth to protest. "I want to know why our guest was here tonight."

Jaz gives a huff, mumbling, "Probably just some low-grade troublemaker."

Ezri crosses her arms. "Nex and I will split the earnings then."

Jaz jumps out of his seat. "What do you mean?"

Her eyes widen, a look of innocence if not for the curl of her lips.

"Let's just say the sooner you get me my information, the sooner we can lighten the load of his pockets."

A winning grin stretches the edges of his mouth. "You got it, Boss." He's halfway out the door before he halts and turns around.

“We haven’t had a name-caller in a while, huh?”

The second-in-command rolls his eyes, muttering about how rules indeed aren't made to be broken, while Ezri merely laughs.

She taps her heel against the floor, adding to the thundering bass of the club beneath. People want to be free, not reminded of their empty little lives."

He shrugs in agreement. "Still...bit harsh, don't you think?"

"I suggest you find the man whose hands I want to cut off – instead of tempting me to take a pair in closer proximity." Her gaze lands pointedly on his ringed fingers.

Jaz holds his palms up in surrender, backing out towards the door. "Alright, alright. Point taken, Boss..."

Then, from behind Nex, he whispers, "Don't miss me too much!"

“I heard that,” Nex grinds out, grumbling something about wishing he couldn’t, as he fiddles with his Amplifier.

With a quiet yelp, Jaz scurries out of the office before Nex can act on the deadly look he shoots.
~

"I'll keep tabs on him," Nex reassures her, eyes shifting to the doorway, where jaunty footsteps echo down the hall. Ezri gives an absent-minded nod. "The system is running the serial number and I'll pull the security feeds."

"Anything else?" Nex asks, quiet, when she doesn't respond.

A crease appears in the space between her brows as she settles behind her desk.

"No"—Ezri presses a finger to her temple—"I just need a moment – a drink – before I deal with the Mods."

He nods, drifting over to the decanter to pour her usual. The glass is set before her. Nex takes his leave without another word.
